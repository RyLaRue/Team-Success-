About Us

This page was created to assist those who have food restrictions find something to eat at different resturants.The map feature was created so that the user can physicly see where the different resturants are located.The facts page gives information on different topics relating to food.The search page is for the user to search a resturant by name and by food restriction.The info page gives more information about the different food restrictions that we cover.The home page gives different pictures of foods of people have a certain food restriction can eat.
