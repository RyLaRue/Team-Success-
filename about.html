About Us

This page was created to assist those who have food restrictions find something to eat at different restaurants.The map feature was created so that the user can physically see where the different restaurants are located.The facts page gives information on different topics relating to food.The search page is for the user to search a restaurant by name and by food restriction.The info page gives more information about the different food restrictions that we cover.The home page gives different pictures of foods of people have a certain food restriction can eat.
