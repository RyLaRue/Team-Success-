Information About Dietary Restrictions

In America today over 15 million people face the problem of trying to find food with a dietary restriction. This page is to help spread the awareness of these problems.

A milk allergy is a an immune reaction to one or more of the constituents of milk from any animal

More Info

Over 18 million people in America suffer from a gluten allergy. Gluten refers to the proteins found in wheat, rye and barley which cause an adverse reaction in people with gluten-related disorders. On a gluten-free diet, these grains and any foods or ingredients derived from them must be removed from the diet.

More Info

Over 7.3 million people are vegetarians in America today. A vegetarian is someone who lives on a diet of grains, pulses, nuts, seeds, vegetables and fruits with, the use of dairy products and eggs.

More Info

Over 2 million people are vegan in America today. A vegan is a person who does not eat or use any animal products.
